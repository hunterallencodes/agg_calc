Instructions are simple! Just enter the size of your mainline drain, choose the units of measurement that you are using, then enter the size of the venting that will be protruding through roof, (additional vents can be added by clicking the Add More Fields button), and then click the Calculate button and a window will pop up letting you know whether the venting is sufficient and the area of the drain and total area of the vents. Click the reset button to start over.
